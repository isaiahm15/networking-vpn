<br><h1 align="center">Virtual Private Networks</br>
<br>
<img src="https://cdn-icons-png.flaticon.com/512/6554/6554321.png" height="200" width="200"/></br>
</h1>
Especially working remotely, connecting securely to your company's network using a VPN is essential. This guide will show you how to setup and install a VPN program.
<br/>


<h2>Program Used</h2>
<p>
 
- <b>[Proton VPN](https://protonvpn.com/)</b>

</p>

<h2>Environment Used </h2>
<p>
 
- <b>Windows 10 Enterprise</b> (22H2)

</p>

<h2>Website Used</h2>
<p>
 
- <b>[What is My IP Address](https://whatismyipaddress.com/)</b>

</p>

<h2>Step-by-Step Overview</h2>
<p>
 
- <b>Observe original IP address on What is My IP address</b>

- <b>Download and install Proton VPN</b>

- <b>Launch ProtonVPN and click "CONNECT"</b>

- <b>Observe new IP address on What is My IP address</b>

</p>

<p>First, open your web browser of choice and go to https://whatismyipaddress.com to observe your IP address before its changed by Proton VPN.
<br><img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0e758a31-a8cf-4129-b891-c1c6fa51c13a" height="75%" width="100%"/>
<br/>
</p>

<h2 align="center">Installation</h2>

<p>Visit https://protonvpn.com and click on "Get Proton VPN". You'll have to sign up with an email if you don't have an account with Proton VPN already.
<br><img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/db08b87a-1eb3-401d-8f19-fa4632b8a67e" height="75%" width="100%"/>
<br/>
</p>

<p>After signing up, your Proton VPN homepage will have a "Download Proton VPN" section available where you can choose and download a compatible version of Proton VPN for your system.
<br><img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c523ec62-84ff-4553-8c85-66b3da25af91" height="75%" width="100%"/>
<br/>
</p>

<p>Once the Proton VPN installer is downloaded, launch and install the program on your device.
<br><img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6dd17a41-6ab5-4fd5-a80a-394fee88ba09" height="75%" width="100%"/>
<br/>
</p>

<h2 align="center">Setup</h2>

<p>Launch Proton VPN and click "Connect" to allow the program to begin encrypting your network traffic.
